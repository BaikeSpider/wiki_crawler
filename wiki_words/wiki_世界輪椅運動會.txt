世界轮椅运动会
世界轮椅运动会，全称是世界轮椅及截肢者运动会，也叫做斯托克·曼德维尔（Stoke Mandeville）轮椅运动会，是一个多项目的残疾人运动会。
第一届运动会是由路德維希·古特曼在1948年举办。
比賽[编辑]

1948: Stoke Mandeville Games for the Paralyzed [1] July 28, 1948, archery competition, 16 competitors[2] (14 men, 2 women[3])
1949–1951: Stoke Mandeville Games
1952:Stoke Mandeville, England – First International Stoke Mandeville Games[4] A Dutch team participated, making it an international event[2]
1953–1959: International Stoke Mandeville Games (2nd–8th)
1960: 9th Annual International Stoke Mandeville Games, Rome, Italy – later known as the 1st Paralympic Games[5]
1961–1963: International Stoke Mandeville Games (10th–12th)
1964: 13th International Stoke Mandeville Games for the Paralysed, Tokyo, Japan – later known as the 2nd Paralympic Games[6]
1965–1967: International Stoke Mandeville Games (14th–16th)
1968: 17th International Stoke Mandeville Games, Tel Aviv, Israel [7] – later known as the 3rd Paralympic Games
1969–1972: International Stoke Mandeville Games (18th–20th)
1972: 21st International Stoke Mandeville Games,Heidelberg, Germany – later known as the 4th Paralympic Games[7][8]
1973–1975: International Stoke Mandeville Games
1976: None (5th Paralympic Year)
1977–1979: International Stoke Mandeville Games
1980: None (6th Paralympic Year)
1981–1983: International Stoke Mandeville Games
1984: None (7th Paralympic Year)
1985–1987: International Stoke Mandeville Games
1988: None (8th Paralympic Year)
1989–1991: International Stoke Mandeville Games
1992: None (9th Paralympic Year)
1993–1995: International Stoke Mandeville Games
1996: None (10th Paralympic Year)
1997–2000 – World Wheelchair Games
2000: None (11th Paralympic Year)
2001–2003: World Wheelchair Games[9]
2004: None (12th Paralympic Year)
2005: World Wheelchair and Amputee Games[10] – Rio de Janeiro,  巴西. Over 700 athletes from 44 nations. Five events: track and field, table tennis, archery, shooting, and billiards.[11]
2006 World Wheelchair and Amputee Games, 班加羅爾,  印度
2007: World Wheelchair and Amputee Games [12] 臺北市,  中華民國

2009: IWAS World Games – 班加羅爾,  印度,[13][14][15]
2011: IWAS World Games – 夏尔迦,  阿联酋 – 1–10 December 2011[16]
2012: None (14th Paralympic Year)
